Cancer Research Context: This file implements the tissue-level organization for cancer simulations, providing methods to create and manage cellular structures that model tumor growth and development. It handles the initialization of the microenvironment, placement of cancer and normal cells, and creation of specific tissue geometries relevant to cancer research. The implementation supports studies of tumor morphology, invasion patterns, and interactions with immune cells.
